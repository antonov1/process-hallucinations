First, **a customer files a complaint about a product or service**. Then, **customer service logs the complaint** and **customer service assigns the complaint to the relevant department for investigation**. Next, **the assigned team reviews the complaint details**. Based on the review, either **the complaint is approved and the customer is notified**, or **the complaint is rejected and the customer is notified**. If a refund is approved, **the financial team processes the reimbursement**. Then, **the complaint is marked as resolved**. Finally, **the customer provides feedback on their satisfaction**.
