First, the customer may be invited to provide post-travel feedback or access additional services. After that, the customer completes their journey. After the journey, the customer receives a reminder, and receives instructions for check-in or boarding. Afterwards, the system updates the seat inventory, sends the ticket via SMS, and sends the ticket via email, after the system generates a ticket. The ticket is then generated. Then, the customer provides both personal information and payment details. Before that, the customer selects the desired date and time. After that, the customer selects the route and the desired time and date. In the end, the customer searches for tickets.
