First, **the department receives a potential customer inquiry about a product or service**. Then, **the sales staff or customer support collects relevant information** and  **the sales staff or customer support addresses any concerns or questions**. If a customer is interested, **the customer is guided through selecting the appropriate product or service**. Next, **the sales representative provides a quota**. After approving the quota, **the customer places an order**, **the order is recorded in the system**, and **the customer receives confirmation of their order**.
