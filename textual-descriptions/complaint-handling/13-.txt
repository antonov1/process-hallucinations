First, the customer may provides feedback on their satisfaction. After that, the complaint is marked as resolved. Then, either a reimbursement is processed or the complaint is rejected and the customer is notified. If a reimbursement is processed, the complaint is then approved and the customer is notified. Afterwards, the complaint details are reviewed. Then, the complaint is assigned to the relevant department. The complaint is afterwards logged. In the end, the complaint is filed.
