First, **a customer searches for a flight, train, or bus ticket**. Then, **the customer selects the preferred route**, **the customer selects the desired date and time**, and **the customer provides personal information**. Next, the customer provides payment details. After the booking is confirmed, **the system generates a ticket**, **the ticket is sent to the customer via email**, and **the ticket is sent to the customer via SMS**. At the same time, **the system updates the seat inventory**. Prior to the journey, **the customer receives a reminder**, and **the customer receives instructions for check-in or boarding**. Finally, **the customer completes their journey**, and optionally, **the customer is invited to provide post-travel feedback or access additional services**.
