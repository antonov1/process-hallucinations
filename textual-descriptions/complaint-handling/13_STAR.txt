First, the complaint is marked as resolved. Then, customer service logs the complaint and the complaint is rejected and the customer is notified. Next, a customer files a complaint about a product or service. Based on the review, either the customer provides feedback on their satisfaction, or customer service assigns the complaint to the relevant department for investigation. If a refund is approved, the complaint is approved and the customer is notified. Then, the assigned team reviews the complaint details. Finally, the financial team processes the reimbursement.
