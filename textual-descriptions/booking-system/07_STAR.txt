First, the customer completes their journey. Then, the customer receives a reminder, a customer searches for a flight, train, or bus ticket, and the system updates the seat inventory. Next, the customer provides payment details. After the booking is confirmed, the customer selects the desired date and time, the customer selects the preferred route, and the customer is invited to provide post-travel feedback or access additional services. At the same time, the customer provides payment details. Prior to the journey, the ticket is sent to the customer via SMS, and the customer receives instructions for check-in or boarding. Finally, the customer provides personal information, and optionally, the ticket is sent to the customer via email.
